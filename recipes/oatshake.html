oatshake Recipe

Description

This is the recipe of the on of the healthiest breakfast containing good amout of protein as well as fibre.A must go breakfast for all professionals

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup nuts (almonds, walnuts, etc.)

steps

  1. In a blender, combine rolled oats, milk, banana, honey, cinnamon powder, and yogurt.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the mixture into a glass and top with nuts.
  4. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
  5. Enjoy your healthy oat shake!
