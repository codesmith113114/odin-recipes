Chicken Recipe

Description

This is the Famous indian chicken recipe which was used by the famous software engineer Aman Raj from india who is known as marcos as his programming stage name This is the dish he used to eat with his friend when he was in college and he used to make this dish for his friends and they used to enjoy it a lot.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp oil

steps

  1. Take a bowl and add chicken, ginger garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, salt, lemon juice and oil.
  2. Mix well and marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
  4. Place the marinated chicken in a baking dish and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice or roti.
